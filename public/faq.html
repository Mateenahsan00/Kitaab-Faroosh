Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Kitaab Faroosh.

General & Account
Kitaab Faroosh is Pakistan's premier campus marketplace. It allows students to buy and sell educational materials like books, notes, and uniforms directly within their own institutions, making it safer and more convenient than general marketplaces.

Buying & Selling
Click the "+ SELL" button in the navbar. Select the appropriate category (Books, Notes, etc.), choose your institution level (School, College, University), and fill in the details. You can upload up to 5 clear photos of the item. After clicking "Post Now", our team will review your ad to ensure it meets community guidelines before it goes live.
For the best results, include the condition of the item (New/Used), the original price vs. your selling price, and a detailed description. For books, mention the author and edition. For uniforms, specify the size and institute name. Clear, well-lit photos significantly increase your chances of a quick sale.
You can use our built-in real-time chat system by clicking "Chat with Seller" on any item page. This allows you to ask questions and negotiate without sharing your personal contact details immediately. You can also view the seller's phone number if they have chosen to make it public.
Absolutely! Most sellers are open to reasonable negotiations. Use the chat feature to discuss pricing. Be respectful and fair in your offers. Remember, the goal is to make education affordable for everyone while ensuring sellers get fair value for their items.
Your ad remains active until the item is sold or you manually deactivate it from your "My Ads" dashboard. If your item sells, please mark it as sold promptly so other buyers know it's no longer available. Inactive ads may be removed after 30 days.
Ads may be rejected if they contain inappropriate content, misleading information, prohibited items, or poor quality images. We review all ads to maintain quality standards. If your ad was rejected, you'll receive a notification explaining the reason. You can edit and resubmit it following our guidelines.
We only allow items related to education and student life. Prohibited items include illegal materials, pirated software, non-educational electronics, weapons, or any items that violate Pakistani law. We also prohibit misleading or deceptive advertisements.
Safety & Trust
Safety is our top priority. We highly recommend meeting in safe, public locations—ideally on your campus during daylight hours. Bringing a friend along is always a good idea. Never share sensitive personal information or financial details over chat.
We strongly advise against paying in advance. Always inspect the item thoroughly to ensure it matches the description before handing over any money. Cash on delivery in a public place is the safest method for both parties.
If the item you receive doesn't match the listing description, contact the seller immediately through chat to resolve the issue. If they're unresponsive or uncooperative, report the incident via "Contact Us" with screenshots. We recommend inspecting items before payment to avoid this situation.
If you find a suspicious ad or a user behaving inappropriately, please report it immediately via the "Contact Us" page. Provide as much detail as possible. We take all reports seriously and will take action, including banning users who violate our community trust.
We use industry-standard security measures including password encryption (bcrypt) and secure sessions. Your personal information is never shared with third parties without consent. We encourage users to keep their contact details private until they're comfortable sharing them with verified buyers/sellers.
Be cautious of: (1) Users asking for advance payment before meeting, (2) Prices that seem too good to be true, (3) Requests for personal banking details, (4) Pressure tactics to make quick decisions, (5) Users refusing to meet in person. When in doubt, trust your instincts and report suspicious behavior.
