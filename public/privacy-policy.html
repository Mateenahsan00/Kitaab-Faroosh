Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register on Kitaab Faroosh, we collect information such as your name, email address, city, and profile details. We also collect item data (descriptions, photos) that you choose to upload.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve the marketplace features.
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers.
  • Ensure site security and prevent fraud.
  • Send important service updates and notifications.

3. Data Visibility

Your name, city, and active listings are visible to all users. Your email address and phone number are only shared with users you choose to interact with via the platform or show contact info features.

4. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures, including password hashing and secure database connections, to protect your personal information.

5. User Rights

You have the right to edit your profile and delete your listings at any time. To request full account deletion, please contact us through our official Support channel.
